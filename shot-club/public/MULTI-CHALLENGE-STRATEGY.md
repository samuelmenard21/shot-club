## Key Strategic Advantages

### 1. **Flexibility**
Users can pick their challenge level (5K, 10K, custom). Higher adoption rate.

### 2. **Dual-Track Growth**
- Steady growth from individual associations (100s of users per month)
- Potential exponential growth from province-wide partnerships (10,000s of users in one week)

### 3. **No Revenue Friction**
Both paths are **completely free**. This removes the #1 objection and makes sales easier.

### 4. **Data Moat**
Every challenge run on your platform = more shot data. Better recommendations. Better predictions. Competitive advantage.

### 5. **Network Effects**
As more associations use your platform, kids naturally compare leaderboards across associations. This drives more signups.
