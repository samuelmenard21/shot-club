## Follow-Up Strategy

1. **First email:** Send pitch
2. **3 days later:** No response? Send one follow-up with "Just checking in — let me know if you have questions."
3. **1 week later:** If still no response, move to next association
4. **If they respond:** Schedule 15-min call or email back with link to partnership page

---

## Subject Lines That Work

✅ **Good (Gets opens)**
- "Free 10K Challenge tracker for [Association]"
- "Make your summer challenge easier"
- "Better way to track your 10K challenge"

❌ **Bad (Gets deleted)**
- "Partnership opportunity"
- "New hockey app"
- "Check this out"

---

## Key Selling Points

When they ask questions, emphasize these:

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| "How much does it cost?" | Completely free. Forever. No hidden fees. |
| "How hard is setup?" | We handle it. 24 hours, done. One link to share. |
| "Will parents use it?" | Yes — it's easier than Excel sheets and kids love the live leaderboard. |
| "What if families prefer paper?" | Both work. Offer the app but paper tracker also works. |
| "Do kids need accounts?" | No. Parents sign in once with Google, kids tap to log. |

---

## Expected Response Rate

Typical association outreach:
- **Email open rate:** 30-40% (subject line matters)
- **Response rate:** 10-20% of opens
- **Close rate:** 70-80% of responders (solution is free and solves a real problem)

**Target:** Email 20 associations, expect 2-4 to say yes.

---

## After They Sign Up

1. **Day 1:** Welcome email with leaderboard link
2. **Day 2:** Follow-up offering to help with family communication
3. **Week 4:** Check-in on how it's going
4. **End of summer:** Collect success story (how many families used it, engagement metrics)

Success stories become your best marketing — use them on the partnership page.

---

## Questions to Ask When They Sign Up

"To make sure we set this up right:"
1. How many families do you expect?
2. What dates is the challenge (start/end)?
3. Your association name (for branding)?
4. Who should we send the leaderboard link to?
